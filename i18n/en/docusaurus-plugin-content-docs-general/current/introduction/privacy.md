We may collect certain user information (including personal information and/or sensitive personal information) in the following ways:

- We collect the information you provide directly to us including when you visit our website, register.
- For example, we collect information when you interact with Apix, register as a Apix user, create or modify your profile, interact with your Apix online account, access and use the Apix Hub, participate in any interactive feature of the Services, participate in a Apix sponsored event, apply for a role at Apix, or request customer support or communicate with us via third-party social media sites.

Please keep in mind that the information you choose to provide in your Apix profile may also be considered as Personally Identifiable Information or be considered as sensitive information that can identify you as an individual.
Information we collect in your use of the Services
When you access Apix we may automatically collect information as you use the Services. The type of information that we capture includes but may not be limited to the following:
- Usage information: We monitor user activity connected with the Services and may collect information about the features and functionality used, the APIs uploaded or downloaded.
- Login information: We log information about you when you access and use the Apix Hub including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, access times, browser types and language, Internet Service Provider (ISP)
- Device Information: We collect information about the device that you log into the Services from, including the hardware model, operating system and version, and the unique device identifiers
- Information collected by cookies and other tracking technologies: We use various technologies to collect information, which may include saving cookies to your computer. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve the Services and your user experience and preferences, allowing you to access and use the Services.
- Third-party cookies: If you sign up for access to Apix using a third-party service, those third-party services may be using cookies that are subject to those third-party services’ own rules and policies. Apix does not control the use of third-party cookies and other tracking technologies.
Information we collect from other sources
Apix may also obtain information from third parties and combine that with information that we collect via the Services.  Any access that we may have to such information from a third-party service is in accordance with the privacy notice and authorization procedures determined by that third-party service. We protect data obtained from third parties in accordance with the practices described within this Privacy Policy, plus any additional restrictions imposed by the source of the data.
